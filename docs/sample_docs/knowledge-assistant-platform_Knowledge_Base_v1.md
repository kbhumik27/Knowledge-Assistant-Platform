# NMCG Knowledge Base – Version 1

## 1. About NMCG

The National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG) is the implementation arm responsible for conservation, protection, rejuvenation, and management of the River Ganga and its basin. Its vision includes restoring the wholesomeness of the river through continuous flow (Aviral Dhara) and unpolluted water quality (Nirmal Dhara).

---

## 2. Objectives of NMCG

* Effective abatement of pollution in River Ganga.
* Conservation and rejuvenation of River Ganga.
* Maintenance of ecological flows (E-Flows).
* Sustainable river basin management.
* Public participation and stakeholder engagement.
* Biodiversity conservation and ecosystem restoration.

---

## 3. Namami Gange Programme

Namami Gange is the flagship integrated conservation mission approved by the Government of India for cleaning and rejuvenating River Ganga. It focuses on pollution reduction, river conservation, ecological restoration, and community participation.

---

## 4. Key Pillars of Namami Gange

1. Sewerage Treatment Infrastructure
2. River Front Development
3. River Surface Cleaning
4. Biodiversity Conservation
5. Afforestation
6. Public Awareness
7. Industrial Effluent Monitoring
8. Ganga Gram Development

---

## 5. Five Guiding Concepts

* Nirmal Ganga (Clean Ganga)
* Aviral Ganga (Continuous Flow)
* Jan Ganga (Public Participation)
* Gyan Ganga (Knowledge & Research)
* Arth Ganga (River-linked Economic Development)

---

## 6. Major Intervention Areas

### Sewage Management

Construction and operation of sewage treatment plants (STPs), interception and diversion of drains, and wastewater treatment projects.

### Industrial Pollution Control

Monitoring and regulation of industrial effluent discharge into the river system.

### River Surface Cleaning

Removal of floating waste and pollutants from river surfaces.

### Biodiversity Conservation

Protection of dolphins, turtles, wetlands, fisheries, and aquatic ecosystems.

### Afforestation

River basin plantation and ecological restoration activities.

### Community Participation

Awareness campaigns, Ganga Praharis, educational initiatives, and stakeholder engagement.

## 7. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is NMCG?
A: NMCG is the implementation authority responsible for Ganga rejuvenation and management.

Q: What is Namami Gange?
A: It is the flagship river conservation programme for pollution abatement and rejuvenation of River Ganga.

Q: What are the main pillars of Namami Gange?
A: Sewerage infrastructure, riverfront development, river surface cleaning, biodiversity, afforestation, awareness, industrial monitoring, and Ganga Gram.

Q: What is meant by Aviral Ganga?
A: Maintaining continuous ecological flow in the river.

Q: What is Arth Ganga?
A: Connecting economic development with river conservation and community participation.
